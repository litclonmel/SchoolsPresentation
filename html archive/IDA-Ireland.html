LIT Limerick School of Art & Design


  • Limerick School of Art & Design (LSAD) is one of three Faculties in Limerick Institute of Technology (LIT)
  • The largest Art and Design School outside of Dublin (over 1300 students).
  • LSAD traces its roots back to 1852.
  • Outstanding international reputation and also ranked among the top 50 fashion schools in the world.

Programme Offerings

The following programmes are offered in LIT Clonmel:
  • B.Sc. (Honours) in Creative Media & Design
  • B.Sc. (Ordinary) in Creative Media & Design
  • B.Sc. (Honours) in Digital Animation Production (Level 8)
  • B.Sc. (Honours) in Game Art & Design (Level 8)
  • B.Sc. (Honours) in Visual Effects (Level 8) (TBC for 2019)
  • All programme have embedded level 6 & 7 exit awards
  • Level 6 & 7 graduates can apply for level 8 add on awards

Why B.Sc. Programmes?

  • All programmes offered by LSAD at LIT Clonmel are Bachelor of Science degree programmes
  • Because of the amount of technological skills developed (including coding skills), the programmes are designated as Bachelor of Science programmes
  • No prior knowledge of technology is assumed
  • Two of the programmes are unique in being the only B.Sc. programmes in Ireland in the Game Art and Animation fields






Why a Portfolio?


  • In addition to academic ability (Leaving Cert or FETA), a portfolio allows applicants to demonstrate a creative capability, which is beneficial for a career in the Creative Media sector
